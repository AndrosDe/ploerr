Water

Water is the main ingredient of beer, accounting for 93% of its weight.
In Plörrbräu it is accounting for 95% (97% by Plörrbräu Lau).

Though water itself is, ideally, flavorless, its level of dissolved minerals, specifically, bicarbonate ions, does influence beer's finished taste.

Due to the mineral properties of each region's water, specific areas were originally the sole producers of certain types of beer, each identifiable by regional characteristics.

Plörrbräu is brewed with water form the local Natural Reserve: Tibaum.
